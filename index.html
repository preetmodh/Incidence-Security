About Us

Incidence Security is a modern and innovative cybersecurity company dedicated to protecting businesses and individuals from cyber threats. We provide comprehensive security solutions, including network monitoring, penetration testing, VAPT and SOC.

Our Services

  • Network Security

    We safeguard your network infrastructure, ensuring secure data transmission and protection against unauthorized access.

  • Penetration Testing

    Our experts perform thorough penetration tests to identify vulnerabilities in your systems and recommend effective countermeasures.

  • VAPT

    VAPT is a comprehensive security testing process that helps identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in your computer systems, networks, and applications. It involves both manual and automated techniques to assess the security posture of your infrastructure.

  • SOC

    A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized unit that monitors, detects, and responds to cybersecurity incidents and threats. SOC services include continuous monitoring of networks and systems, threat intelligence analysis, incident response, and vulnerability management.
