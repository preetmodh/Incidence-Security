About Us

Incidence Security is a modern and innovative cybersecurity company dedicated to protecting businesses and individuals from cyber threats. We provide comprehensive security solutions, including network monitoring, penetration testing, VAPT, and SOC.

Our Services

  • Network Security

    We safeguard your network infrastructure, ensuring secure data transmission and protection against unauthorized access. Our network security services include:

    • Firewall implementation and management
    • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
    • Virtual private network (VPN) setup
    • Wireless network security
    • Security audits and risk assessments

  • Penetration Testing

    Our experts perform thorough penetration tests to identify vulnerabilities in your systems and recommend effective countermeasures. Our penetration testing services include:

    • External and internal vulnerability assessments
    • Web application testing
    • Mobile application testing
    • Social engineering assessments
    • Reporting and remediation guidance

  • VAPT

    VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing) is a comprehensive security testing process that helps identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in your computer systems, networks, and applications. It involves both manual and automated techniques to assess the security posture of your infrastructure. Our VAPT services include:

    • Comprehensive vulnerability scanning
    • Exploitation testing
    • Threat modeling and risk assessment
    • Security posture evaluation
    • Remediation assistance

  • SOC

    A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized unit that monitors, detects, and responds to cybersecurity incidents and threats. SOC services include continuous monitoring of networks and systems, threat intelligence analysis, incident response, and vulnerability management. Our SOC services include:

    • 24/7 security monitoring
    • Real-time threat detection and response
    • Log management and analysis
    • Incident investigation and remediation
    • Vulnerability management and patching
